    Make a website for free

    How to make a free site in simple steps

    Set up the basics

    Before you make you website you must follow these 12 steps:

    1. Create an account on replit.com

    2. Create an account on cloudflare.com

    3. Create an account on github.com

    4. Purchase a domain. Free domain names are available from freenom.com

    5. Log in to Replit by going to repl.it and clicking the "Log In" button.

    6. Create a new project on Replit by clicking "Create REPL" button at the top left.

    7. In the menu, select "HTML" as the template and name it whatever you want.

    8. Connect the project to git: Log into Github in your current  browser window. In the Replit project, scroll down to the bottom of the menu at the left of the page and click Git. At the right of the page click "Create a git repo" then click "New Repo". If you are asked a question click "Yes" or "Continue". Connecting to github may be broken or buggy, so be patient.

    9. Go to github.com and verify you have successfully created a new repository with your site.

    10. Log into dash.cloudflare.com, click "Pages" at the left of the screen.

    11. Click "Create Project" and "Connect to Git". Select your new repo and click "Begin Setup". Finally, scroll down and click "Save and Deploy".

    12. Congratulations! You have created project synced with Replit! Click "Continue to Project" at the bottom right.

    Finish setting up your site.

    1. Once you have a project on cloudflare, go to "Pages" and click your site name e.g"my-site".

    2. Next, click "Visit site" at the right, It should display "Hello world". If it doesn't, double check your steps or contact me. If you need further help or documentation, go to developers.cloudflare.com

    3. To get your site on google, type "Site: yourdomain.xyz" into the search bar.

    4. Click the first search entry, you will be brought to a page.

    5. Enter your domain name in the right(not left) box. You must enter the whole name, not the short name. E.g. "https://www.yourdomain.xyz" instead of "yourdomain.xyz".

    6. You will be asked to verify your ownership of the site.

    7. Download the html file the page tells you to download, then drag the file into the Replit project.

    8. Now you must wait. It often takes months for sites to start showing up on google, you can speed up search results by posting your site on social media, or by spreading the word.

    9. Click here for more help.

    Set up a custom domain

    1. Navigate to your project as indicated in "Finish setting up your site".

    2. Click "Custom Domains", then "set up a custom domain", enter the domain name you own and press enter.

    3. Click "Begin DNS transfer", and reenter your domain name(e.g. abc.com) and press enter in the new tab. Select a plan for your site(I recommend free) and click "Continue".

    4. It will ask you about DNS records, simply scroll down and click "Continue".

    5. Next log into your domain registrar (e.g. Namecheap, Freenom) and change the name servers listed on the Cloudflare page. If you need help, read Cloudflare's instructions on developers.cloudflare.com, or look up "How to change nameservers on [your registrar(like namecheap)]" on google search.

    Design your Site

    1. Congratulations! you have created your first site!

    2. Now it is time to actually design your website.

    3. You can use Wix to make your website, or code it yourself with HTML/CSS/JS.
